Club History

Club formed

Falkenbergs FF is formed in 1928, on a Tuesday, January 3, when Falkenbergs Gymnastiksällskap closes down its football section. Georg "Jija" Torstenson, who took the initiative to start the club, becomes FFF's first chairman.

Unbeatable record

September 27, FFF's reserve team beats Tvååker's A team with 21-1. Curth "Dalles-Curth" Nilsson scores 17 (!) of the goals and thus sets an almost unbeatable goal record. During seven seasons in the FFF, "Dalles-Curth" scored a total of 142 goals.
